Deluxe Comfort Bed

What is Used to Make It

  • Material for Fireplace: High-quality ceramic logs with LED lighting to simulate flames.
  • Armchair Materials: Durable fabric upholstery with high-density foam for comfort, supported by a sturdy metal frame.
  • Table Construction: Fiber-reinforced concrete for the base with a natural orange pigment finish.
  • Rug Composition: Thick, soft synthetic fibers designed for high traffic areas.

Its Features

  • Fireplace: Electric unit with adjustable heat settings and a realistic flame effect that can be used with or without heat.
  • Armchair: Ergonomically designed with a contoured shape to support posture and relieve back pain.
  • Table: Weather-resistant and suitable for both indoor and outdoor use; minimalist design that complements a variety of decor styles.
  • Rug: Hypoallergenic, easy to clean, and resistant to stains.

Pricing

  • Fireplace: Approximately $500 - $700, depending on size and features.
  • Armchair: Ranges from $250 - $400, based on upholstery and design complexity.
  • Table: Available for about $150 - $300, influenced by size and material quality.
  • Rug: Typically priced between $100 - $200, varying with size and material type.
