Pothos Plant (Epipremnum aureum)

What is Used to Make It

  • Frame Material: The Pothos is housed in a pale blue plastic pot that offers durability and lightweight handling. The choice of a pale blue color gives a calm and soothing visual appeal, which complements the vibrant green of the leaves.
  • Support: Strong wooden slats that provide excellent mattress support without the need for a box spring.
  • Plant:: This Pothos features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that cascade in trailing vines. The leaves display a rich green color, characteristic of healthy Pothos plants, and they can grow quite long if properly cared for..

Its Features

  • Air Purifying One of the most celebrated features of the Pothos is its ability to purify the air. It absorbs and removes common household toxins such as formaldehyde, benzene, and xylene from the air, making it not only a decorative but also a health-beneficial addition to any room.
  • Aesthetic Appeal:With its lush, trailing vines, the Pothos adds a touch of nature to indoor spaces. It can be placed on shelves where its vines can drape gracefully downward, or in higher spots like the tops of cabinets for a cascading effect.
  • Versatility in Placement::Thanks to its adaptability to different light conditions and its aesthetically pleasing look, the Pothos can be placed in various locations around the home or office, including areas with low natural light.

Pricing

  • Variability: Price may vary based on size and wood type selected.
  • Additional Costs: Shipping and handling fees may apply, and prices may be higher if purchasing from specialty or boutique stores.
